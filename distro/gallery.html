May 2020

Retort Cooling Water System Upgrade

At a well-known pet food manufacturer in Atlantis, Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering designed and implemented an improvement to the existing cooling water system. Originally the retort cooling water circuit was in series with the cooling tower water circuit.

After adding an additional retort, and during warm ambient temperatures, the client struggled to cool down the retorts and reduce the temperature of the water in the bulk tank, resulting in slower batch cooling times on the retorts.

We designed and implemented a separate cooling tower water circuit with its own dedicated pump. This resulted in the cooling water circuit to the retorts to become a separate circuit and the contents of the bulk water tank is now cooled constantly by the dedicated circuit to the cooling towers. This simple improvement resulted in far better batch times and improved efficiency on retort production.

May 2020

Compressor Station

At a well-known food manufacturer in Montague Gardens, Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering corrected a fundamental problem with the pipe sizing between the compressors, filters, dryer and air receiver.

The client had sufficient compressor capacity installed, but due to incorrect pipe sizing in the compressor station, there was an air volume shortage in the plant where the retorts were installed – fed by a line of ± 300m in length. By simply upsizing the pipework and header in the compressor room, the problem was solved and the VSD controlled compressor now runs at a noticeably lower speed, which results in an electrical power saving.
