New Upmarket Clothing Factory

A Cape Town exclusive upmarket clothing manufacturer moved its entire factory from Salt River to Epping at the end of 2019/beginning 2020. Jaclin Engineering was awarded the tender for:

  • Design and manufacture of the full steam and condensate reticulation system to supply 110 stations on the Trouser, Shirt and Jacket manufacturing lines.
  • The steam pressure required in the factory necessitated a full pressure reducing station, which was also designed and supplied by us.
  • Design and manufacture of the full vacuum reticulation system to supply 99 stations on the Trouser, Shirt and Jacket manufacturing lines.
  • Design and manufacture of the full compressed air reticulation system and ringmain to supply 187 stations on the Trouser, Shirt and Jacket manufacturing lines, as well as the Cutting Room.
  • Re-location of 2 x compressors and 4 x vacuum pumps from Salt River.
  • Supply and installation of a new air dryer and after-filter.
  • Design and full installation of both new compressor and vacuum pump rooms.
  • Jaclin Engineering worked closely with the TWI International consultants on the factory layout and process flow, as well as providing CAD support.

Hotwell

At a well-known animal feeds manufacturer in Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering designed, manufactured and installed an 8000 litre 3CR12 hotwell tank for their boilers. The tank is equipped with state-of-the-art ultrasonic level control, a sparge pipe and direct steam-injection heating.

This turnkey project included the design and casting of the concrete bases, the design of the 3m high stand and catladder, as well as the tank. The tank and stand were manufactured in our workshop, lagging and cladding was fitted and installation and tie-in on site was all completed on one day.

Steam Header

At a well-known pet food manufacturer in Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering designed and manufactured a fully certified steam header for their boiler house, to replace a rotten 20- year old one. The new header was built to the exact dimensions of the old one, so as to minimise the need for pipework modifications.

The new header was equipped with a full set of Zetkama isolating globe valves and an Ari steam trapset, which Jaclin Engineering holds the agency for as a distributor.

Compressor Room

At a well-known towel manufacturer just outside Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering redesigned and corrected the pipework in their compressor room. We also re-located an existing compressor and air receiver, as well as installed a new dryer and pre and after filters, as well as exhaust ducting.

Currugated Cardboard Manufacturer

Central Vaccum System

At an upmarket brand clothing manufacturer in Atlantis, Jaclin Engineering designed and installed a new central vacuum system for the factory. The old system that was in use, was fabricated with undersized PVC piping, which resulted oin poor vacuum pressure at the ironing and pressing stations as well as static electricity buildup in the pipes. Jaclin’s new correctly sized mild steel piping system resulted in uniform suction pressure throughout the production floor, resulting in better finished and production efficiencies.

Chimney Stack

At a well known pet food manufacturer in Cape Town, Jaclin Engineering conducted thickness testing on both the existing boiler chimney stacks, by use of a mobile crane to access the full height of the chimneys. The coal boiler stack was found to be severely corroded and the wall thickness very thin towards the top. Jaclin Engineering manufactured and installed a new chimney within a short space of time, preventing a potential disaster of the old chimney collapsing.
